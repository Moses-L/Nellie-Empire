Our Story

At Nellie Empire, food is more than nourishment, it is a bridge to the heart. Rooted in rich Nigerian heritage, our kitchen brings you the authentic Taste of Home in every plate.

Every meal is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, traditional spices, and a professional touch that ensures every bite is a celebration of flavour. From your daily comfort meal to your grandest celebration, we show up, consistently and deliciously.

Based in the heart of Gwarimpa, Abuja, we serve individuals, families, and corporates who deserve nothing but the best.
